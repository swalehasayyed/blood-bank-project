Welcome to the Blood Donation Management System

This system is designed to streamline the process of blood donation by providing easy access to donor and receiver information, enabling blood searches, and handling enquiries efficiently.

Features of the Blood Bank

Donor Registration

Register as a donor to help save lives by providing blood to those in need.

Blood Requests

Request blood from available donors based on your specific blood group and urgency.

Enquiries

Get answers to all your queries about the donation process, eligibility, and more.
